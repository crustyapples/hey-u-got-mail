Our Story

We've seen the headlines. They're stressful. It's also difficult not seeing our loved ones as frequently, and even harder to imagine how others affected by COVID-19 are coping. That's why we created the Hey, You Got Mail! Project to spread positivity in these unprecedented times.

The Hey, You Got Mail! Team sources, designs, makes and mails high quality handmade cards on your behalf to be sent to someone you love, or to a community you want to support as #sgunited.

There's always something special about receiving a handmade card in the mail, and we can't wait to share the love with you.

Spread some Positivity!

For every card you send, we send one to an elderly!

Multiple Languages!

We support translation to Chinese, Malay and Tamil!

Save the planet!

All your cards are delivered in recycled envelopes!
