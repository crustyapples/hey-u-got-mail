The Hey, You Got Mail! team is proud to announce our first partner nursing home - Ling Kwang Home for Senior Citizens!

Back in the day, the home started off as a clinic providing free and heavily subsidized medical clinics to residents. In December 1983, it established itself as one of Singapore's nursing homes. Today, it has evolved to become one of the biggest nursing homes in Singapore, with a capacity of 350 residents.

With every card purchase, the Hey, You Got Mail Team! will send another to friendly seniors from Ling Kwang Nursing Home.

Together, let's spark a moment of joy and brighten up their days!
